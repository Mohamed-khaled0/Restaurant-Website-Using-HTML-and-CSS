Our Story

Over 25 Years of Culinary Excellence

At Gourmet Guru, our passion for exquisite flavors and exceptional dining experiences has driven us for over a quarter-century. Founded in 1998 in the heart of 6th of October, Egypt, we have been dedicated to bringing the best of global cuisine to our cherished guests.

Our team of talented chefs meticulously crafts each dish using the freshest ingredients, ensuring every meal is a masterpiece. Whether you're joining us for a casual dinner or a special celebration, Gourmet Guru promises an unforgettable gastronomic journey.
